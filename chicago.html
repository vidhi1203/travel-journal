Getting around

The practical bit.

From the airport

Blue Line from O'Hare runs 24/7 into the Loop, ~45 min. Get a Ventra card at the station.

In the city

The L covers almost everything; downtown is genuinely walkable if you dress for the wind.

Worth knowing

Lake-effect cold is no joke in December — layers, and gloves you can use a phone with.
