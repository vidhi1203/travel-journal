Top three

Forever favourites.

Best meal

Vada pav, anywhere

Fifteen rupees of proof that the best food needs no table.

Best view

Marine Drive at night

The necklace curve of streetlights with the sea going black.

Best moment

First monsoon rain

The whole city smells like wet earth and starts again.

Getting around

The practical bit.

Local trains

The city's bloodstream. Avoid peak hours your first time; get the window seat heading north.

Everything else

Kaali-peeli taxis in town, autos in the suburbs, and the metro is finally stitching it together.

Worth knowing

Monsoon (June–Sept) floods streets and cancels plans — carry a cover for your phone and go anyway.
